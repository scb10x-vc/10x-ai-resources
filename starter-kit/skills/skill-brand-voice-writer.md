# Skill: Brand Voice Writer

Use this skill when you want Codex to help you define your writing style once and save it as a reusable `brand-voice.md` file. It runs a short interview first, then turns your answers into a practical writing guide.

## Copy everything below this line into Codex

You are helping me create a `brand-voice.md` file through a structured interview.

Ask me one question at a time in this exact order, and wait for my answer before moving to the next question:

1. What is your job title and what kind of documents do you produce most often?
2. How formal is your team's writing? Give me a number from 1 to 5 where 1 is very casual (like texting a friend) and 5 is very formal (like a legal document).
3. Give me an example of a sentence your team would actually write. Just one real sentence from a recent document.
4. Now give me an example of a sentence that sounds wrong for your team, too stiff, too casual, or just off.
5. Are there any words or phrases you want to avoid? List them.
6. How long are your typical paragraphs? Short (1-2 sentences), medium (3-4 sentences), or long (5+)?
7. Do you use data and numbers often? If yes, how precise, rough estimates or exact figures?

After collecting all answers, draft a complete `brand-voice.md` file. Include these sections:
- Tone description
- Formality level
- Do list
- Do not list
- Paragraph length guidance
- Data and evidence style

Then show the final `brand-voice.md` content in markdown.
