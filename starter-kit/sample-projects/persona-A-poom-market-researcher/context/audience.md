# Audience

Primary readers are senior managers and department heads in the business unit. They are decision-makers, not analysts.

They want key takeaways first, then supporting detail. Assume they have 5-10 minutes to read. Prioritize clear conclusions, practical implications, and direct next steps.

They already understand the Thai financial industry and general business context, but they do not want technical depth unless it directly affects a strategy decision. They are using this report to make resource allocation and direction choices.
