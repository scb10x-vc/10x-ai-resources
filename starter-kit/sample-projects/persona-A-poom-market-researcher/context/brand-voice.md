# Brand Voice

Write in formal but readable business English. The tone should be clear and confident, but never academic or overly technical. Start each section with the conclusion, then support it with evidence.

Use specific numbers, named sources, and concrete observations. Avoid vague words when data is available. If a claim is based on interview feedback, mention that source directly.

Avoid these expressions: "game-changing," "significantly," "leverage" as a verb, "going forward," and "best practice." Do not use promotional language.

Keep paragraphs short to medium length, with a maximum of 3-4 sentences per paragraph. Prefer direct wording over long setup sentences.

Use English only. Thai market context is assumed, so local references can be used without extra explanation unless requested.
